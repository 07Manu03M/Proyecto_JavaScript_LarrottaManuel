The world of Dungeons and Dragons

Welcome to Dungeon and Dragons

In a universe where magic, darkness, and glory intertwine, the bravest heroes and the most feared creatures are born. Here you don't just create characters... you create legends. Dungeon & Dragons is a fully interactive and visual character creation platform, where you decide the fate of your adventurers. Will you be a dragon-defying warrior, an elven sorceress who wields the elements, or a rogue master of stealth?

What can you do in Dungeon and Dragons?

  • 1. Create an epic character from scratch.
  • 2. Choose from races and classes from the official world of Dungeons & Dragons.
  • 3. Customize your stats: strength, intelligence, dexterity, wisdom, charisma...
  • 4. Choose weapons, armor, and magical accessories.
  • 5. Add unique abilities such as spells, special powers, and charms.
  • 6. Give your character a unique name, gender, and appearance.
  • 7. Save your favorite creations to view later or continue editing.

How does it work?

  • 1. Enter the character creator.
  • 2. Use the filters to select race, class, equipment, and skills.
  • 3. Customize to the max with names, accessories, and stats.
  • 4. Save your character to the list to access its full details.

What is the objective?

Dungeon and Dragons is a platform for players, content creators, and role-playing fans who want to design characters for their games, stories, or just for fun.

Accessible from anywhere

Whether you're on a PC, tablet, or mobile device, Dungeon and Dragons runs seamlessly on any device, with no complicated installation or setup required.

Why use Dungeon and Dragons?

  • 1- Modern and friendly visual with an immersive experience.
  • 2- Access to official information on D&D races, classes, and skills.
  • 3- Hundreds of possible combinations to customize your character.
  • 4- Saving system so you don't lose your creations.
  • 5- Compatible with role-playing campaigns, board games or interactive stories.
